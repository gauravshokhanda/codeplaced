Amalfi Acquisition 
Problem Statement
In our initial conversations with the client, the leadership team highlighted growing operational challenges that were beginning to affect both internal efficiency and management visibility. As their virtual workforce expanded across multiple roles such as County Leads, Supervisors, and Skip-Tracers, managing performance, payroll, and work tracking through manual spreadsheets and disconnected systems became increasingly difficult.
Disconnected Operational Data
Task progress reports, payroll inputs, employee ratings, and historical records were being maintained across separate Google Forms and legacy Excel files. This fragmentation made it difficult to maintain consistency and resulted in frequent reconciliation issues between past and current datasets.
Manual Payroll Calculations
Payroll calculations for different team roles were being computed manually using static spreadsheets. This created dependency on manual checks, increased the risk of human error, and significantly slowed down monthly payroll cycles.
Lack of Performance Visibility
Employee ratings and task completion data existed but were not structured in a way that enabled monthly trend analysis or performance benchmarking. Management lacked a reliable mechanism to track contribution levels across team members over time.
Time-Intensive Reporting
Preparing consolidated reports required manually cleaning raw form responses, integrating them with previous records, and calculating compensation figures for each file and role. This process consumed valuable administrative time and delayed strategic decisions.

Solution
Our goal was to design a centralized, automated Excel-based workflow that could transform raw operational data into actionable management insights while reducing reliance on manual effort.
Centralizing Data Across Systems
We integrated historical operational records from legacy payroll files with live Google Form submissions into a unified Excel environment. This created a single source of truth for all workforce-related activities including task tracking, compensation, and performance ratings.
Automating Payroll Workflows
Dynamic calculation engines were developed within Excel to automatically compute compensation for County Leads, Supervisors, and Skip-Tracers based on task completion and performance criteria. This eliminated the need for repetitive manual calculations and improved payroll accuracy.
Building Performance Rating Systems
Monthly rating dashboards were implemented to monitor individual team contributions over time. Automated aggregation logic enabled management to evaluate trends in productivity and identify top-performing team members without requiring manual report generation.
Tracking Workforce Activity
Interactive dashboards were designed to visualize task progress, last activity dates, and submission frequency across all virtual assistants. This allowed leadership to proactively identify inactive contributors and ensure accountability across distributed teams.
Integrating Legacy Records
To maintain continuity with previously completed files, historical data from older systems was incorporated into automated workflows. Existing payroll entries could override system-generated values where needed, ensuring bookkeeping accuracy without disrupting automation.

Technical Implementation
Data Integration & Cleaning
Raw Google Form responses were processed using advanced Excel functions including FILTER, QUERY, REGEXMATCH, MMULT, and array-based lookup logic to standardize and clean incoming operational data.
Automated Payroll Engine
End-to-end compensation logic was implemented using dynamic formulas capable of calculating role-based payouts across multiple files. These workflows ensured real-time updates as new submissions were received.
Performance Aggregation
Monthly employee ratings were calculated automatically by extracting date-time values from form submissions and grouping them into monthly reporting periods. This enabled accurate trend analysis without manual intervention.
Interactive Dashboards
Custom dashboards were created to display:
Employee Performance Ratings


Payroll Status (Due vs Paid)


Task Completion Progress


Last Activity Monitoring


File-Wise Compensation Breakdown


Advanced conditional logic and data validation controls were incorporated to improve usability and reduce reporting errors.

Business Impact
Reduced Administrative Effort
Automating payroll and reporting workflows significantly minimized manual spreadsheet manipulation, allowing management teams to focus on operational strategy rather than data preparation.
Improved Payroll Accuracy
Dynamic compensation calculations reduced discrepancies between bookkeeping records and calculated payouts, ensuring fair and transparent payment tracking across all roles.
Enhanced Workforce Visibility
Leadership gained real-time visibility into team activity levels, submission consistency, and monthly performance trends, enabling proactive management of distributed virtual assistants.
Faster Decision-Making
By consolidating fragmented operational data into a single reporting environment, the client was able to generate insights on task progress and payroll status without time-consuming manual reconciliation.
Continuity with Historical Data
Integration of legacy records ensured that previously completed files were accounted for within automated workflows, maintaining financial accuracy while transitioning to a scalable reporting system.

Through the implementation of automated Excel workflows and interactive dashboards, the client successfully transitioned from manual data handling to a structured, insight-driven operational model. The result was improved payroll transparency, stronger performance monitoring, and greater efficiency in managing a growing virtual workforce.
